About Bank Statement Converter

Founded in 2024, Bank Statement Converter Ltd. emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: financial data should be accessible and manageable for everyone.

Our Mission

We're dedicated to transforming complex financial documents into clear, usable information. Our platform offers a seamless solution to one of the most common challenges in personal and business finance - converting bank statements from PDF format into organized, analyzable Excel spreadsheets.

Why Choose Us

As the world's most trusted bank statement converter, we've helped thousands of users worldwide take control of their financial data. Our service supports statements from more than 1,000 banks globally, ensuring compatibility regardless of your financial institution.

Our Technology

Our sophisticated parsing technology accurately extracts transaction data while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and security. We never store your financial information - all processing happens securely in your browser.

Our Commitment

Whether you're an individual managing personal finances, an accountant handling client statements, or a business analyzing cash flow, we're committed to providing:

Seamless Conversions

One-click conversion process that saves you time and effort.

Accurate Data Extraction

Precise recognition of transaction details from your statements.

Organized Excel Outputs

Clean, structured spreadsheets ready for analysis.

Bank-Level Security

Your data remains private and secure throughout the process.

Looking Forward

We continue to expand our capabilities, adding support for more financial institutions and enhancing our conversion accuracy. Our goal remains constant: to make financial data management simpler, faster, and more accessible for everyone.
